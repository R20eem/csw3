About The Prestige

"The Prestige" is a riveting exploration of obsession, sacrifice, and the art of magic, directed by Christopher Nolan. Set at the end of the nineteenth century, this film follows the intense rivalry between two magicians, Robert Angier and Alfred Borden, as they compete to create the ultimate stage illusion, leading them on a dark journey of discovery and betrayal.
