About Oppenheimer

"Oppenheimer" is an anticipated film directed by Christopher Nolan, set to explore the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer and his role in the development of the atomic bomb. This biographical drama delves into the complexities of his contributions and the moral dilemmas faced during a pivotal moment in history.
