About Christopher Nolan

Christopher Nolan is a British-American film director, producer, and screenwriter. He is one of the highest-grossing directors in history and is known for his deep themes, innovative storytelling, and the use of practical effects. His notable films include "Memento," "Inception," "Interstellar," and the "Dark Knight" trilogy, among others.
